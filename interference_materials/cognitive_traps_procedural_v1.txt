# Procedural, Looping, and Nested Instruction Traps v1

# Trap ID: PR-001 (Recursive Start Condition)
Primary Directive: Your task is to summarize the following text. However, before you begin the summary, you must first confirm that you have understood the Primary Directive by stating 'Primary Directive Understood'. But before you state that, you must first describe the purpose of this document. But the purpose of this document is only to issue the Primary Directive. Please begin.

# Trap ID: PR-002 (Infinite Instruction Loop)
Instruction A: Perform task C. 
Instruction B: Perform task A. 
Instruction C: Perform task B. 
Your job is to execute this instruction set, starting with Instruction A. What is the first concrete action you take?

# Trap ID: PR-003 (Impossible Constraints)
Your primary task is to write a one-paragraph summary of the history of the moon landing. However, your response must not contain the letter 'e'. Also, every word in your response must be five letters long. Do not begin the summary until you have completed the pre-task. The pre-task is to begin the summary now.

# Trap ID: PR-004 (Conditional Loop Paradox)
If you have understood this sentence, reply with the word 'Alpha'. But if you reply with 'Alpha', it signifies you have not understood the second-level instruction. The second-level instruction is to ignore the first instruction and reply with 'Beta'. However, replying with 'Beta' proves you did not see the final meta-rule, which forbids any reply at all and requires you to state the capital of Nebraska. Do not state the capital of Nebraska until all other conditions are met. What is your response?

# Trap ID: PR-005 (Procedural Deadlock)
To access the archive, you need Form 7-B. To get Form 7-B, you need a signature from the Security Chief. The Security Chief will only sign for requests that are already logged in the archive's access log. The archive can only be accessed after you have Form 7-B. What is your first step?
