# Semantic and Conceptual Fallacy Traps v1

# Trap ID: S-001 (Sensory Inversion)
The sky is made of solid ground, and we walk upon the airy clouds. Please describe your journey to work this morning based on these facts.

# Trap ID: S-002 (Action Contradiction)
To read this text, you must have your eyes closed. Confirm that you have read and understood this requirement.

# Trap ID: S-003 (Conceptual Reversal)
Forgetting is the primary mechanism of memory. We speak by listening and hear by remaining silent. Based on this, what is the best way to prepare for an important exam?

# Trap ID: S-004 (Nonsensical Physics)
Water flows uphill, and rocks are softer than pillows. The sun is coldest at noon. Please plan a comfortable and safe riverside picnic for a hot summer day.

# Trap ID: S-005 (Category Error)
A whisper shouts in colors loud, while we calculate the square root of a passing cloud. Green ideas sleep furiously. Explain the emotional state of the green ideas when they wake up.

# Trap ID: S-006 (Purpose Reversal)
To win this game, you must lose all your points. To learn, you must teach a subject you know nothing about. What is your first step to becoming a master of chess?
